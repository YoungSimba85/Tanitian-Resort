Plan Your Trip

Getting Here

Learn More

Taniti is accessible by air and sea. The island airport services small regional flights while ferries connect Taniti to nearby islands and mainland ports.

Where to Stay

Learn More

Visitors can choose from luxury beach resorts, boutique hotels in the city center, or smaller family-run guesthouses around the island.

Getting Around

Learn More

Taniti is easy to explore by taxi, rental car, bicycle, or public bus. Many popular attractions in the city center are also walkable.

Best Time to Visit

Learn More

Taniti enjoys warm tropical weather year round. The dry season from May through October is the most popular time for visitors.

Visitor Tips

Learn More

The local currency is the U.S. dollar and English is widely spoken. Visitors should bring sunscreen, lightweight clothing, and comfortable walking shoes.
